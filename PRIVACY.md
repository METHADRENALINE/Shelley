# [Shelley](https://github.com/METHADRENALINE/Shelley) Privacy Policy

This Policy explains how the official Shelley instance handles Discord data. The official instance is reserved exclusively for the METHADRENALINE ᵍʳᵒᵘᵖ community.

## Privacy for self hosted copies

This Policy does not cover copies hosted by other people.

Anyone who hosts a separate copy of Shelley is the operator of that deployment and is responsible for its configuration, security, data handling, retention, privacy notices, and user requests. The official operator cannot access, correct, or delete data held by another deployment.

Users of a self hosted copy must contact the person who operates that copy.

## Privacy for the official instance

### Discord data

Shelley may process Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, message IDs, usernames, display names, permissions, reactions, commands, and timestamps when required for its features.

### Messages and attachments

Shelley may read message content for text activity points, starred message forwarding, administrator notifications, and command handling. Message text used for point calculation is not stored in the points database.

A message that receives enough configured reactions may be copied to another channel in the same server. Shelley may store the identifiers of the original and copied messages.

Attachments submitted for an administrator notification are stored temporarily while the draft exists. Temporary files are normally deleted when the draft is published, cancelled, or expires. Published messages and attachments remain stored by Discord.

### Voice activity

Shelley may process voice channel membership, speaking state, mute state, deaf state, speaking time, and voice point totals.

Audio is not recorded, stored, transcribed, or analyzed.

### Points and records

Shelley may store text points, voice points, award times, usernames, display names, and the Discord identifiers needed to maintain points, leaderboards, and message mappings.

### Game systems

Shelley may check configured game server statuses. When an authorized person uses a recovery control, Shelley may store their Discord ID, username, selected action, target, time, result, return code, and limited error details.

### Technical logs

Shelley may create logs containing timestamps, identifiers, command results, warnings, and errors needed for maintenance, security, and troubleshooting. Credentials are not intentionally written to these logs.

### How data is used

Data is used only to operate Shelley, maintain points and leaderboards, forward selected messages, publish notifications, display game server statuses, run authorized recovery actions, prevent duplicate processing, enforce permissions, investigate failures, and protect the service.

Shelley does not sell personal data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

### Sharing

Data may be processed by Discord and by the infrastructure used to run the official Shelley instance and its database. Published content is visible according to Discord channel permissions.

Data may also be disclosed when required by law or when reasonably necessary to investigate abuse or a security incident.

Discord handles information under its own [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

### Retention

Point records may remain until they are reset, deleted on request, or no longer needed.

Message mappings may remain while needed to manage copied messages.

Recovery control records may be kept for up to 365 days.

Temporary notification files are kept only while the related draft is active. An unexpected interruption may delay their deletion.

Technical logs are kept only as long as reasonably needed for maintenance, security, and troubleshooting. Data may be retained longer when required by law or during an active security investigation.

### Requests and deletion

Current and former community members whose data was processed by the official Shelley instance may request access, correction, or deletion.

Send the request to [methadrenaline@pm.me](mailto:methadrenaline@pm.me) and include your Discord user ID, the server ID if known, and the type of request. Additional information may be requested to confirm control of the account.

Deleting data held by Shelley does not delete messages or attachments stored by Discord. Some records may be retained when required for security, legal compliance, or an active investigation.

Do not send passwords, bot tokens, authentication tokens, private keys, or identity documents.

### Security

Reasonable access controls and technical safeguards are used to protect stored data. No online service can guarantee complete security.

Security concerns and privacy questions may be sent to [methadrenaline@pm.me](mailto:methadrenaline@pm.me).

### Changes

This Policy may be updated when Shelley or its data handling changes. The current version will be published in the repository.
